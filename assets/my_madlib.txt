Make Me A Video Game!

I the purple and blue Sam have was Sarah's swift sister and plan to steal her tall puppies!

What are a tiger and backpacking mouse to do? Before you can help Sarah, you'll have to collect the purple cats and stormy trees that open up the 3 worlds connected to A Peter's Lair. There are 7 trees and 7 clouds in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
